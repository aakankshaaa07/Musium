bluetooth enabled

Tired of entagling your wires? So were we, and this is why our new musium has inbuilt bluetooth. You can pair it with your wireless earbuds, car stereo or speakers and enjoy your favourite songs as you like without any discomfort. This version is compatible with almost every device and features LE Power Control and LE Audio.
